general information

We're typically very private and not very open at all, even with close friends and other people we generally trust. This isn't personal, it's just because of past experiences and problems that we currently face.

You're generally fine to refer to any of us as 'Moon', especially if you don't know who's fronting- we're also fine with 'they/them' pronouns and are bodily 18.

When interacting, be gentle and don't treat us like shit.

We use 'we/us' to refer to ourselves, but more often we use 'I' to refer to ourselves collectively and each as individuals (sorry in advance if that's ever confusing).

am i allowed to know who's fronting?

If you want to know who's fronting, we have it displayed on our website and closer friends can easily get our SP username. If you're not sure, please ask- but also keep in mind that we might sometimes want to keep this private for whatever reason. If we do, please respect this and don't be pushy.
