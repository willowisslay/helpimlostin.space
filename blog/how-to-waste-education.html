How to waste 2 years of education in one easy step

So, here I am once more! Recently I've returned from a 3 day trip which went horribly after the first day, but that's a story for another blog post. I've also been in college education studying graphic design, and last week (Friday, a week ago exactly) was the submission date of my final project which is worth a significant amount of my grade.

However I may have had a bit of mental block for the last few weeks and got a very limited amount of work done when I needed to just lock the fuck in, and instead ended up submitting nothing. I don't know what happens next, I don't know if I get given some kind of extention or if that's just it. I know I should continue doing work, I just don't know where to start in time for Monday. I feel so lost, I feel so fucked, I feel like I've just thrown away two years of college education all because I didn't use the support available to me and speak up more when I knew I was struggling.

Do not try this at home. Fuck.
