Spain, projects and an end

Hi! Hello! Hai! Hewwo! *fucking dies*

I'm back! I feel like it's been so long since I made a blog post and then I checked and it wasn't even a month ago since I did my last one. Oh well!!! Here I am, I got back from Spain a few days ago it was very nice. Here's a photo I took!

And yeah. I don't know what else to say really, I did kind of write the title before writing so it just feels like I'm crossing off a checklist of stuff to write about lol.

I've felt fine about it for a while, now feeling even better about it- whatever that means. I found it really difficult to navigate and figure things out. I'm much more content with where I'm at with it all.
